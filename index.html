Teq Vault LLC is a solo-founded studio in Bossier City, Louisiana. Every tool here was built to solve a real problem — not to have a product page. TeqDocs started because no offline reader handled all three formats cleanly. TeqSlice exists because slicing sprite sheets in Photoshop costs money and requires installation. TeqCarve because data recovery tools either cost hundreds or have terrible UX.

Same engineering discipline that goes into the games. Fewer features than the big tools, but the right features, working offline, for free.
